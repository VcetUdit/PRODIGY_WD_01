About Us


Welcome to FriendlyPaws - Where Pet Love Meets Culinary Delight!


At FriendlyPaws, we've created a haven where pet enthusiasts and food lovers come together to celebrate the joy of companionship. Nestled in the heart of Mumbai, our pet cafe is more than just a place to grab a cup of coffee; it's a vibrant community that cherishes the special bond between humans and their furry friends.
